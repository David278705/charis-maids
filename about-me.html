About charis

Authentic Cleaning Services

At the age of 16, I came to the United States full of dreams and determination. From a young age, I had a natural passion for cleanliness and organization—values deeply instilled in me by my beloved grandmother. Her influence sparked in me a genuine appreciation for maintaining order and creating welcoming, well-kept spaces. As I navigated life and pursued my education, I was blessed to cross paths with a remarkable mentor, Miriam. It was through her mentorship that I acquired the detail needed to provide an exceptional service and quality control that focused on client satisfaction.

Excellence has been one of the key virtues of my job and it reflects on the passion displayed through my cleaning and the labor provided. These past 24 years, altogether with my team, I have given my best effort to offer an outstanding service.

My family has always been my motivation and I hope to leave them a legacy that highlights perseverance and excellence through my entrepreneurial spirit!
